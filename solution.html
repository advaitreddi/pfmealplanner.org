Community Support

Join or create healthy eating groups at school or in your community. Share recipes, tips, and motivation with peers making better food choices.

Practical Tips

  • Plan meals in advance
  • Learn basic cooking skills
  • Read nutrition labels
  • Shop the perimeter of grocery stores
  • Prepare snacks at home
  • Stay hydrated with water

Making Sustainable Changes

The key to reducing processed food consumption is making gradual, sustainable changes to your eating habits. Start with small steps.

School and Home Environment

Create supportive environments that make healthy choices easier:

  • Keep healthy snacks readily available
  • Remove tempting processed foods
  • Get family members involved
  • Pack nutritious lunches

Reading Food Labels

Learn to identify and avoid:

  • Added sugars and artificial sweeteners
  • Trans fats and hydrogenated oils
  • Artificial preservatives and colors
  • Excessive sodium content
